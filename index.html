About Me

I am currently an Assistant Professor of Mathematics at Pitzer College. In 2024, I completed my Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics at Cornell University where I was fortunate enough to be co-advised by Francesca Parise and Lindsay Anderson. Prior to joining Cornell in 2018, I received my Bachelors degree in Mathematics from Trinity College. My research leverages tools from network theory and dynamical systems to study emergent behavior in interconnected systems. Outside of research, I am passionate about teaching and have received graduate teaching awards from both the Department of Mathematics (2020) and the Department of Computer Science (2021) at Cornell University.
