About Me

My name is Shriya Nagpal and I am an Assistant Professor of Mathematics at Pitzer College. Before joining Pitzer in Fall 2025, I earned my Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from Cornell University. My research leverages tools from network theory and dynamical systems to study synchronization in networks of coupled phase-oscillators. Outside of research, I am passionate about teaching and have been honored with graduate teaching awards from both the Department of Mathematics (2020) and the Department of Computer Science (2021) at Cornell.
